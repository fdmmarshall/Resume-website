About

I hold two Bachelor's degrees in Linguistics and the German language from the University of California, Berkeley, and two associate's degrees in German Studies and English Literature from Palomar College.

I am half Salvadorian and half Portuguese. My husband is a Former Marine Infantry Sergeant. And we have a rambunctious adorable two year old son, named Felix. I enjoy learning languages and am passionate about the study of syntax and semantics.

My career goal is to continue to strengthen my skills for a technical role. A career as a front-end developer and UX designer offers me the ability to combine my love of linguistics and language with an outlet for creativity and design.
